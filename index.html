For HTML Assignment - MY HOBBY, I'll be showing you my favorite hobby: programming. This is something I've enjoyed doing ever since taking your TEJ1O class in my Grade 9 year. I have a lot to talk about in terms of programming and my passion for it, even though it's only been about a year since starting it.

Unlike other hobbies of mine in the past, this one is different to me. Playing instruments, sports, and video games are all simply leisurely activities that I don't have an interest to pursue as a career. Although, it would be nice to think about it, considering that they can be very enjoyable. I think this is what sets apart programming for me, though. It's something that I plan on learning in college, and then doing in the real world as a job. They say to enjoy what you do for a living, and I think that'll be the case.

However, as of right now it is a very fun hobby of mine. It doesn't earn me any money just yet, but I still find it quite enjoyable and interesting. It intrigues me due to its depth of knowledge I can get from doing it. Solving problems with programming is really what keeps me going. Figuring out a difficult coding question in Python after long trial and error is very rewarding, and so is finishing a large project of mine. Even just doing these assignments posted on Schoology bring a sense of fulfillment. It's for this reason, that I've decided to pursue doing all three of the options listed on Schoology for Unit 3.
